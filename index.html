Realtime Tracking

Proof of Delivery

Signature capture or pin verification to ensure shipments are delivered to intended customers

Multi-Hub Support

Vehicle Management

Know your vehicle status, and set reminds for its scheduled maintenance

Expense Management

Custom Report

FEATURES

TASK UPDATE

Dispatchers can update their task from point of pickup to point of delivery.

POOL

Dispatchers can choose task nearest to them, which they can execute quickly, for example picking up a Package closest to dispatchers location.

INSTANT MESSAGING

Notify dispatchers with the instant messaging features, which can also work offline and is absolutely free.

EXPENSE RECORD

Dispatchers can record expense spent on vehicle management, be it maintenance or repairs.

WORK HISTORY

Dispatchers can view work history for better records and logistics reports.

SCAN

Dispatchers can scan packages picked up and to be delivered.
